## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### Company Overview

**Shot On Me** is a revolutionary social payment platform that combines peer-to-peer money transfers with venue-based spending and social networking features. The platform allows users to send money to friends for use at participating venues, discover friends nearby, and share moments—all while keeping funds secure in an escrow account until redemption.

### Mission Statement

To create a seamless, social, and secure way for people to share experiences and money at venues, making every moment more meaningful and every transaction more personal.

### Value Proposition

**For Users:**
- Send money to friends instantly via phone number
- Discover friends and venues nearby
- Share moments on social feed
- Secure escrow system—funds held safely until use
- No app required for recipients (SMS-based)
- Tap-N-Pay virtual cards for easy venue spending

**For Venues:**
- AI-powered analytics and insights
- Automated promotion management
- Customer engagement tools
- Revenue growth through social payments
- Real-time analytics dashboard
- Subscription-based premium features

**For Platform Owner:**
- Complete business management through owner portal
- Venue approval and management system
- Transaction monitoring and compliance
- Revenue from transaction fees and venue subscriptions
- Scalable technology platform

## 2. COMPANY DESCRIPTION

### Business Model

**Shot On Me** operates as a money transmitter with a unique escrow-based model:

1. **User Funding:** Users add funds via credit/debit card (processed through Stripe)
2. **Escrow Holding:** All funds held in business escrow account until redemption
3. **Peer-to-Peer Transfers:** Users send money to friends (funds remain in escrow)
4. **Venue Redemption:** Users spend funds at venues via Tap-N-Pay virtual cards
5. **Revenue Generation:**
   - Transaction fees (2-3% on transfers)
   - Venue subscription fees ($50-$500/month)
   - Premium features for venues
   - Advertising revenue (future)

### Legal Structure

- **Entity Type:** LLC (Indiana)
- **Licensing:** Indiana Money Transmitter License (required)
- **Federal Registration:** FinCEN MSB Registration (required)
- **Compliance:** AML/KYC program, transaction monitoring

### Competitive Advantages

1. **Social Integration:** First platform to combine payments with social networking
2. **Venue-Focused:** Exclusive focus on venue-based spending
3. **AI-Powered Analytics:** Advanced AI for venue insights and optimization
4. **No App Required:** SMS-based system for recipients
5. **Secure Escrow:** Funds held safely until use
6. **Location-Based:** Discover friends and venues nearby
7. **Owner Control:** Complete business management through owner portal
