## üìç STRATEGIC DECISION: WHERE TO INCORPORATE?

### Option 1: Texas LLC (Recommended) ‚≠ê

**Advantages:**
- ‚úÖ Lower costs (no state income tax)
- ‚úÖ Business-friendly regulations
- ‚úÖ Lower money transmitter license costs ($1,000-$5,000 vs. higher in some states)
- ‚úÖ Faster license processing
- ‚úÖ Better for future fundraising

**Considerations:**

- May need to register as "Foreign LLC" in Indiana if doing business there
- **Recommendation:** Form in Texas, register as Foreign LLC in Indiana

### Option 2: Indiana LLC

**Advantages:**
- ‚úÖ Local (easier to manage)
- ‚úÖ No foreign registration needed
- ‚úÖ Local connections

**Disadvantages:**
- ‚ùå Higher costs (state income tax)
- ‚ùå Potentially higher license costs
- ‚ùå Less attractive for investors

**Recommendation:** **Texas LLC with Foreign LLC registration in Indiana**

## üéØ VENUE ACQUISITION PLAYBOOK

### The Pitch (30-Second Version)

"Hi, I'm [Your Name] with Shot On Me. We're a new app that lets people send money to friends specifically for use at venues like yours. It's like Venmo, but the money can only be spent at participating venues. 

**Benefits for you:**
- New customers (people discover you through the app)
- Increased foot traffic
- Easy payment processing
- Free marketing (we promote you in the app)
- No upfront costs (we only take a small commission when money is spent)

**We're launching in Indianapolis and looking for 10-15 founding venue partners. Would you be interested in learning more?"

### The Full Pitch (5-Minute Version)

1. **Problem:** "People want to send money for experiences, but current apps don't focus on venues."

2. **Solution:** "Shot On Me lets people send money that can only be spent at venues like yours."

3. **How It Works:**
   - Users send money to friends
   - Money is held in escrow
   - Recipients use tap-and-pay card at your venue
   - You get paid, we take small commission

4. **Benefits:**
   - New customer acquisition
   - Increased foot traffic
   - Marketing platform
   - Easy to use (normal card reader)
   - No upfront costs

5. **What We Need:**
   - You sign up (takes 10 minutes)
   - We set up your account
   - You start accepting payments
   - We promote you in the app

6. **Incentives:**
   - Free first month
   - Lower commission (5% vs. 7%) for founding partners
   - Featured placement in app
   - Co-marketing opportunities

### Follow-Up Materials

- One-page info sheet
- Demo video (2-3 minutes)
- Testimonials (as you get them)
- Case studies (as you get them)
