## üéØ EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### The Opportunity

**Shot On Me** is a social payment platform that combines peer-to-peer money transfers with venue discovery and social networking. Unlike traditional payment apps (Venmo, Cash App), Shot On Me restricts spending to participating venues, creating a unique value proposition for both users and venue owners.

### The Problem

- **For Users:** No easy way to send money specifically for venue experiences (drinks, food, events)
- **For Venues:** Limited tools to engage customers, drive foot traffic, and increase revenue
- **Market Gap:** No major player focuses on venue-specific payments with social features

### The Solution

Shot On Me enables users to:
- Send money to friends for use at venues
- Discover nearby venues and friends
- Share experiences through social feed
- Use funds via tap-and-pay virtual cards (no app required for recipients)

### Financial Strategy

**Recommended: Hybrid Bootstrap-to-Funding**
- **Phase 1 (Months 1-6):** Bootstrap $75K ‚Üí Launch in Texas ‚Üí Validate product-market fit
- **Phase 2 (Months 7-12):** Seed Round $350K ‚Üí Scale to 5-10 states ‚Üí Reach profitability
- **Phase 3 (Year 2-3):** Series A $3M ‚Üí National expansion ‚Üí Market leadership

**Why This Strategy:**
- Minimizes dilution (30% vs. 45-60% with early funding)
- Maximizes valuation (3-4x better with traction)
- Maintains control during critical early phase
- Faster path to profitability

### Key Metrics (Year 1)

- **Users:** 25,000-50,000
- **Venues:** 100-200
- **Revenue:** $150,000-$300,000
- **Break-Even:** Month 9-12
- **Valuation (if raising):** $3M-$5M

### Investment Required

- **Phase 1:** $75,000 (bootstrap)
- **Phase 2:** $350,000 (seed round, 10-12% equity)
- **Phase 3:** $3,000,000 (Series A, 15-18% equity)

## üíº PRODUCTS & SERVICES

### Core Product: Shot On Me Platform

#### 1. User Mobile App

**Features:**
- **Wallet System:** Track balance, send/receive money
- **Send Money:** Send to friends via phone number
- **Social Feed:** Facebook-style feed with posts, photos, videos
- **Location Discovery:** Find friends and venues nearby
- **Venue Discovery:** Browse venues, see promotions, check-ins
- **Virtual Card:** Tap-and-pay card for venue redemptions

**Key Differentiators:**
- No app required for recipients (SMS-based)
- Funds held in escrow until redemption
- Venue-only spending restriction
- Social features integrated

#### 2. Venue Portal (Web Application)

**Features:**
- **Dashboard:** Analytics, revenue, customer insights
- **Promotions Management:** Create and manage specials
- **Payment Processing:** Redeem codes, process payments
- **Customer Management:** View customer data, preferences
- **Schedule Management:** Operating hours, events
- **Notification System:** Send SMS to customers

**Key Benefits:**
- Increase foot traffic
- Customer engagement
- Revenue growth
- Marketing tools

### Revenue Streams

#### 1. Transaction Fees (Primary)

**Model:** 2.5-2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- **Charged to:** Sender
- **Example:** $50 transaction = $1.50-$1.75 fee
- **Projected:** 60-70% of total revenue

#### 2. Venue Commissions (Secondary)

**Model:** 5-10% of redemption amount
- **Charged to:** Venue
- **Example:** $50 redemption = $2.50-$5.00 commission
- **Projected:** 20-30% of total revenue

#### 3. Premium Subscriptions (Tertiary)

**Model:** $4.99/month per user
- **Features:** No transaction fees, priority support, exclusive deals
- **Target:** 5-10% of users
- **Projected:** 5-10% of total revenue

#### 4. Venue Subscriptions (Future)

**Model:** $99-$299/month per venue
- **Features:** Advanced analytics, promotion tools, customer insights
- **Target:** 20-30% of venues
- **Projected:** 5-10% of total revenue

### Technology Stack

- **Frontend:** Next.js, React, TypeScript
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express, MongoDB
- **Payments:** Stripe Connect, Stripe Issuing
- **SMS:** Twilio
- **Real-time:** Socket.io
- **Hosting:** Vercel (frontend), Render/AWS (backend)
- **Database:** MongoDB Atlas
