## üéØ KEY TOPICS TO DISCUSS

### 1. Money Transmitter License (CRITICAL)

**What to Ask:**
- "Do I need an Indiana money transmitter license for my business model?"
- "What is the exact application process and timeline?"
- "What are the exact costs? (application fee, bond, net worth requirements)"
- "Can I use a bond premium instead of a bond deposit?"
- "What documents do I need to prepare for the application?"

**What to Explain:**
- Your business model: Users add funds ‚Üí money held in your account ‚Üí users send to friends (stays in your account) ‚Üí users spend at venues (money goes from you to venue)
- You issue virtual cards (Tap-N-Pay) via Stripe Issuing
- Funds are held in escrow until redemption
- You want to launch in Indiana only initially

---

### 2. Business Structure

**What to Ask:**
- "Should I form an LLC, Corporation, or other entity?"
- "What state should I incorporate in? (Indiana?)"
- "Do I need to form the entity before applying for the license?"
- "What are the tax implications of each structure?"

**What to Explain:**
- You haven't formed an entity yet (or if you have, bring documents)
- You plan to operate in Indiana initially
- You may expand to other states later

---

### 3. Escrow Account Structure

**What to Ask:**
- "Is a single business account acceptable, or do I need a separate escrow account?"
- "What are the legal requirements for holding user funds in escrow?"
- "What are the reserve requirements?"
- "Can I earn interest on escrow funds?"
- "What happens to escrow funds if I go out of business?"

**What to Explain:**
- You plan to hold all user funds in a single business account (or separate escrow account if recommended)
- Funds stay in your account until users spend at venues
- Users cannot withdraw funds to bank accounts (venue-only spending)

---

### 4. Geographic Restrictions

**What to Ask:**
- "How do I legally restrict operations to Indiana only?"
- "What technical measures are required to enforce state restrictions?"
- "What happens if an out-of-state user slips through?"
- "What are my legal obligations if I discover a violation?"

**What to Explain:**
- You want to launch in Indiana only
- You plan to implement location verification
- You want to block out-of-state users and venues

---

### 5. Virtual Card Issuance

**What to Ask:**
- "Are there additional regulations for virtual card issuance?"
- "Do I need any additional licenses for issuing virtual cards?"
- "Is it legal to restrict cards to specific merchants/venues?"
- "What are the legal implications of card restrictions?"

**What to Explain:**
- You issue virtual cards via Stripe Issuing
- Cards can only be used at approved venues
- Cards are funded from your escrow account

---

### 6. Terms of Service & Legal Documents

**What to Ask:**
- "What must be included in my Terms of Service?"
- "What disclosures are legally required?"
- "What should the Privacy Policy cover?"
- "Do I need a separate Venue Agreement?"
- "What are the key legal protections I need?"

**What to Explain:**
- You need Terms of Service that disclose:
  - Escrow structure
  - No withdrawal policy (venue-only spending)
  - Geographic restrictions (Indiana only)
  - Transaction fees
  - Refund policy

---

### 7. Compliance Requirements

**What to Ask:**
- "What AML/KYC program requirements apply to my business?"
- "What transaction limits should I set?"
- "What are my SAR (Suspicious Activity Report) obligations?"
- "What record-keeping requirements apply?"
- "Do I need to hire a compliance officer, or can I handle it myself initially?"

**What to Explain:**
- You plan to start lean (you handle compliance initially)
- You expect low transaction volume initially (10K-25K users Year 1)
- You want to understand minimum compliance requirements

---

### 8. Federal Requirements (FinCEN)

**What to Ask:**
- "Do I need to register with FinCEN as a Money Services Business (MSB)?"
- "What is the registration process?"
- "What are the ongoing requirements?"
- "What are the penalties for non-compliance?"

**What to Explain:**
- You understand you need FinCEN registration
- You want to understand the process and timeline

---

### 9. Insurance Requirements

**What to Ask:**
- "What types of insurance do I need?"
- "What coverage amounts are appropriate?"
- "What are the costs?"
- "When do I need to obtain insurance?"

**What to Explain:**
- You want to understand minimum insurance requirements
- You're planning a lean launch

---

### 10. Timeline & Next Steps

**What to Ask:**
- "What is the realistic timeline for getting licensed?"
- "What should I do first?"
- "What are the critical path items?"
- "What can I do in parallel?"

**What to Explain:**
- You want to launch in 3-6 months (or your timeline)
- You want to understand the process
