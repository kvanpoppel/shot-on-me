## üí° VENUE PITCH TIPS

1. **Do Your Homework:**
   - Know the venue
   - Understand their customer base
   - Check their social media
   - Read reviews

2. **Personalize:**
   - Mention specific things about their venue
   - Reference their social media posts
   - Show you've done research

3. **Focus on Benefits:**
   - New customers
   - Increased revenue
   - Marketing support
   - Easy to use

4. **Address Concerns:**
   - "It's easy - works with your existing card reader"
   - "No upfront costs - commission only"
   - "We handle all the tech"
   - "You can opt out anytime"

5. **Create Urgency:**
   - "We're only taking 10-15 founding partners"
   - "Special rates for early adopters"
   - "Launch is in [X] weeks"
